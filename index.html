Grocery Strategy

Split across 3 stores to maximize value — Costco for bulk proteins, Sprouts for produce & pantry, Safeway for weekly deals on fresh veg.

Costco · Sam's Club~$115
Bulk proteins, oats, almonds, olive oil — best $/lb
Sprouts · Walmart, Trader Joe's, Whole Foods~$50
Produce, dairy, pantry sauces & canned goods
Safeway · Fry's, Kroger, HEB, Publix~$30
Weekly sales on avocados, fresh veg, spice upgrades
💡 First shop is ~$185–$200 because it includes pantry items (rice, oats, olive oil, spices) that last 4–8 weeks. Ongoing weekly cost drops to ~$70–$90 — just proteins, fresh produce, and dairy.

Stock these once — they're used across dozens of recipes in this plan.

Batch these on Sunday — they unlock flavor across every meal without extra cooking time.

🌶 Chipotle Lime Crema
Ingredients: 1 chipotle pepper + 2 tbsp adobo sauce + ½ cup Greek yogurt (plain) + juice of 1 lime + pinch of salt
Use on: Rice bowls, chicken tacos, scrambled eggs, salmon
~½ cup · keeps 7 days in fridge
🌿 Lemon Herb Tahini Sauce
Ingredients: 3 tbsp tahini + juice of 1 lemon + 1 tsp garlic powder + 2 tbsp water + 1 tbsp olive oil + dried dill/parsley + salt
Use on: Salmon topping, wrap spread, salad dressing, dipping sauce
~½ cup · keeps 10 days in fridge
🥢 Sesame Ginger Drizzle
Ingredients: 2 tbsp soy sauce + 1 tbsp sesame oil + 1 tsp honey + ½ tsp ginger powder + 1 tsp lime juice
Use on: Stir fry glaze, rice bowl drizzle, salmon marinade, cold noodle sauce
~¼ cup · keeps 2 weeks
🥜 Thai Peanut Sauce
Ingredients: ¼ cup peanut butter + 2 tbsp soy sauce + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp honey + ½ tsp sriracha + 2–3 tbsp warm water to thin
Use on: Chicken rice bowls, noodle dishes, lettuce wraps, dipping sauce for veggies
~½ cup · keeps 2 weeks in fridge
🌱 Chimichurri
Ingredients: 1 cup fresh parsley (packed) + 3 garlic cloves + 2 tbsp red wine vinegar + ½ tsp red pepper flakes + ½ tsp oregano + ⅓ cup olive oil + salt
Use on: Grilled chicken or steak, roasted veggies, egg bowls, spread on wraps
~¾ cup · keeps 2 weeks in fridge
🍜 Miso Ginger Dressing
Ingredients: 2 tbsp white miso + 1 tbsp rice vinegar + 1 tsp sesame oil + 1 tsp honey + ½ tsp ginger powder + 1 tbsp soy sauce + 2 tbsp water
Use on: Grain bowls, roasted broccoli or carrots, cold noodle salads, salmon glaze
~⅓ cup · keeps 2 weeks in fridge

End-of-Week Rescue Meals

When the fridge looks thin and you want to reach for DoorDash — try these instead:

Egg Fried Rice
Leftover rice + 3 eggs + soy sauce + sesame oil + anything in the fridge. 10 min.
Tuna Pasta
Pasta + canned tuna + olive oil + lemon + red pepper flakes. Fancy-tasting, 15 min.
Bean & Cheese Quesadilla
Canned black beans + cheese + tortilla. 8 min. Add chipotle crema.
Protein Scramble Wrap
Eggs + whatever veg remains + cheese + tortilla. The "clean out the fridge" MVP.
Greek Yogurt Power Bowl
¾ cup Greek yogurt + banana slices + almonds + honey + cinnamon. 3 min, 20g protein.
